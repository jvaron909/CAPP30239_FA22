All charts are based on information taken from Washington Post.

Homicides by Month

In 2015, more than 1,500 people were shot and killed by police officers in the United States. The following chart shows the monthly variation in homicides. All months have significantly high levels, above 100 killings. March and July were the months with the highest number of victims, while June has the lowest number.

Top 10 of Cities with the larges number of Homicides

The top 10 cities with the highest number of homicides include many of the most populated cities, as well as, cities with high levels of crime. However, it would be interesting to compare the police homicide rate with other data such as population, number of police officers and the level of crime in the cities.

Number of Homicides by Race and Gender

In terms of race, white males are the most affected group, which is plausible considering that they are also the largest population group in the United States. The same is true for African Americans and Latinos, the second and third most representative population groups in the United States. In terms of gender, the affectation falls disproportionately on men.
